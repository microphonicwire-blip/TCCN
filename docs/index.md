## How to Read Records

Each record includes:
- **Date** — when the incident or event occurred
- **Severity** — rated Low / Medium / High
- **Status** — Open, Closed, or Under Review
- **Involved** — linked people or incidents

## Severity Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 🟢 Low | Minor infraction, no lasting impact |
| 🟡 Medium | Notable offense, may have consequences |
| 🔴 High | Serious offense, active consequences |
