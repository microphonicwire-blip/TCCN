## Summary

During Federal Gamers' second annual April Fools operation, the individual known as **Audon** secretly orchestrated a National Socialist-themed server takeover, while deliberately planting evidence to frame **Joshua Baloni (Oceanwave)** and **Undex** as the responsible parties. The operation caused significant community disruption, resulted in multiple bans, Doge's resignation as Co-Leader, and a mental health crisis for the original server owner.

## Section I — Background & Origins

Audon contacted **Moon** via direct message to plan the April Fools operation, proposing an authoritarian governance model. Moon agreed and redesigned the server under a National Socialist aesthetic — doing so in cooperation with Audon, notably in place of Undex.

Audon then operated the **Fish Gang administrative account**, staging its activity to create the false impression that Undex was its operator. Throughout the operation, Audon performed ignorance of his own involvement and projected apparent distress — while having already arranged for swift server restoration to maintain the illusion of innocence.

:::warning Deliberate Framing
Audon intentionally left Moon without exculpatory evidence, ensuring Moon could not easily defend himself or expose Audon's involvement.
:::

The account **"Oceanwave"** was confirmed to belong to Joshua Baloni. The **Fish Gang Management account** was operated by Audon, who staged its activity to implicate Baloni — with sufficient credibility that the majority of members accepted it as genuine.

## Section II — FG v. Joshua Baloni

Following the incident, **Doge** moved to defend Joshua Baloni after planted evidence had positioned both Baloni and Undex as the primary wrongdoers. After several days of negotiations with Audon, Doge proposed a supervised reinstatement — an "ankle monitor" arrangement. Audon initially refused but ultimately accepted.

Upon reinstatement, Baloni disclosed the full account of events to the community. Due to Audon's planted evidence, however, his testimony was largely disbelieved — only **Doge** and **Undex** acknowledged its credibility.

Shortly thereafter, Baloni was banned from the server without stated cause — an action attributed to Audon. This directly resulted in **Doge resigning** from his position as Co-Leader.

## Aftermath

- Baloni was banned without cause shortly after his reinstatement
- Doge resigned as Co-Leader in protest
- Audon manipulated departure records to make bans appear as voluntary exits
- A sustained misinformation campaign successfully positioned Baloni as the responsible party in the eyes of most Fish Gang members
- The original owner of Federal Gamers experienced a mental health crisis as a result of these events
- As of filing, the majority of Fish Gang members hold Federal Gamers and Joshua Baloni responsible
